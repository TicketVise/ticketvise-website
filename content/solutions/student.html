Benefits for students.

Asking questions is a big part in being a student. We want to maximize the ability for students to learn by improving the experience of asking questions. By making a platform that is personal and flexible, we encourage students to ask questions and follow these up if they want to know more.

Personal and accessible.

Creating a ticket is starting a conversations instead of asking a question. Responding to answers from the teaching team leads to a discussion with the assignee for a more personal conversation. Seeing who participated on the question shows the connection between you and the teaching team.

Ordered and structured.

Add labels to your ticket to organize your questions. Labels can be matched with assistants by the coordinator, this makes sure that fellow students with the same questions get consistent answers. Other students can also be given access to the ticket by sharing. Not needing to forward an answer and enabling shared students to ask for more elaboration directly saves time and effort, but increases clarity.

Swift and Sound.

Organizing the process for the teaching team guarantees an answer on the question. The response time is minimized due to the streamlined process. Giving students a quicker answer gives them more time to work on the assignments.
