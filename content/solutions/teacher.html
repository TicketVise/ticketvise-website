Benefits for teachers.

As a teacher, the ultimate goal is to teach students. Answering questions and discussing with students is a big part of this. To focus on this, we made a tool for you which is streamlinedand efficient.

Increase efficiency with automation.

Divide the work amongst your assistants with automatic scheduling. Assigning tickets to assistants resolves any issues of unanswered or double answered tickets, whilst keeping work from your schedule.

Help your students with statistics.

Seeing the labels that are used frequently, you can give general explanations to help all students. Finding times at which most students are active can help predict when questions are asked and when your team needs to be ready to help students swiftly.

Reduce hassle with internal staff discussions.

Being able to discuss tickets with only the teaching team, removes external communication platforms. Be notified when someone mentions you in a staff discussion to help each other and reduce response times.
